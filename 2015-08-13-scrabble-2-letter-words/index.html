Scrabble Tutorial: 2-Letter Words

If you know about the 2 letter words, move on. If you don't, MEMORIZE THEM ALL.

2-letter words are very useful if you want to link your tiles with the tiles on board, and gain a few extra points. It can wreck people who don't know them (obviously).
